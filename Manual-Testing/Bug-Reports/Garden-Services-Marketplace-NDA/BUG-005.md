# BUG-005 - Service price does not match the defined default price in the requirements

| Field | Details |
|---|---|
| **Module** | UI / Service Pricing |
| **Severity** | Critical |
| **Priority** | Critical |
| **Reproducibility** | 100% |

---

## Preconditions
User is authorized and logged in to the system

---

## Expected Result
Based on the requirements, the default price of the service should be $3.0/m²

---

## Actual Result
The price of the service displayed on the UI is $3.5/m²

---

## Impact
> This is a critical bug with direct financial consequences for both customers 
> and the business. When a service is displayed at a higher price than defined 
> in the requirements, customers are being overcharged without their knowledge. 
> This not only violates user trust but also creates potential legal and 
> compliance risks for the platform.
