# BUG-002 - Notification Toggle Buttons Are Not Clearly Visible Due to Insufficient Contrast

| Field | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Module | Settings / Notifications |
| Category | UI / Accessibility |
| Severity | Medium |
| Priority | Medium |
| Reproducibility | 100% |

---

## Preconditions

- User is logged into the application.
- User is on the Settings section.

---

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Open the application.
2. Navigate to **Settings**.
3. Open the **Notifications** section.
4. Observe the notification toggle switches.
5. Attempt to identify whether individual notification options are enabled or disabled.

---

## Expected Result

Notification toggle switches should have sufficient visual contrast between their ON and OFF states.

Users should be able to easily distinguish whether notifications are enabled or disabled without confusion.

The design should comply with accessibility and usability standards.

---

## Actual Result

The notification toggle switches have insufficient color contrast, making it difficult to determine their current state.

Users may be unable to distinguish whether a notification setting is enabled or disabled.

This negatively impacts usability and accessibility.

---

## Impact

Users may unintentionally leave notifications enabled or disabled because the current state of the toggle is unclear.

The issue may lead to missed notifications, unwanted notifications, and overall confusion regarding notification preferences.
