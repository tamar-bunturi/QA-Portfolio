# BUG-002 - AI Chat bot recommends out-of-stock product as available alternative

| Field | Details |
|---|---|
| **Module** | AI Chat / Product Recommendations |
| **Category** | Functional |
| **Severity** | High |
| **Priority** | High |
| **Reproducibility** | 100% |

---

## Preconditions
User is on the platform and has access to the AI Chat functionality

---

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the web application
2. Start a chat with the AI Bot 
3. Send a message requesting a specific product
4. When prompted for capacity, type "5kg"
5. Wait for the bot's response where it confirms 5kg is out of stock and explicitly suggests 6kg as an available alternative
6. Type "6kg" to follow the bot's recommendation
7. Observe the bot's response

---

## Expected Result
The bot should check product availability before recommending items. It should only suggest in-stock products, or clearly mention that the item is out of stock before the user selects it

---

## Actual Result
The bot first suggests 6kg as an available alternative when 5kg is out of stock. After the user selects 6kg, the bot responds that 6kg is also out of stock and redirects to 7kg models, directly contradicting its own previous recommendation

---

## Impact
> This bug exposes a critical flaw in the AI chat system's ability to validate 
> real-time stock availability before making recommendations. When a bot 
> confidently suggests a product as available and then immediately contradicts 
> itself, it severely damages user trust in the AI system. This issue 
> suggests the AI is not properly integrated with live inventory data.
