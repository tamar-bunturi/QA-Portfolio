# BUG-002 - AI Assistant incorrectly reports available Shopify product as unavailable

| Field               | Details                                 |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| **Module**          | Shopify Integration / AI Product Search |
| **Category**        | Functional                              |
| **Severity**        | High                                    |
| **Priority**        | High                                    |
| **Reproducibility** | 100%                                    |

---

## Description

The AI Assistant incorrectly informs customers that a product is unavailable or cannot be found, even though the product exists in the Shopify store catalog and is available for purchase.

---

## Preconditions

1. AI Assistant is enabled and connected to the Shopify store catalog
2. A product exists in the Shopify store and is available for search
3. Customer chat widget is accessible

---

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Open the customer-facing AI Assistant chat
2. Ask about a product that is available in the Shopify catalog
3. Wait for the AI Assistant to process the request
4. Review the AI Assistant's response
5. Verify the product exists and is available in the Shopify store

---

## Expected Result

The AI Assistant should correctly identify products that exist in the Shopify catalog and provide accurate product information.
Customers should receive responses that reflect the actual store inventory and product availability.

---

## Actual Result

The AI Assistant responds that it cannot find the product or indicates that the product is unavailable.
However, the product exists in the Shopify store catalog and can be verified manually, resulting in incorrect information being presented to customers.

---

## Impact

> Customers may receive inaccurate product availability information, potentially leading to lost sales, reduced trust in the AI Assistant, and an overall poor shopping experience.
