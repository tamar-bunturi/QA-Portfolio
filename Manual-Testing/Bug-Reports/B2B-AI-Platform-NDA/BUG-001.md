# BUG-001 - AI Search returns irrelevant Samsung models for specific query "Samsung S23"

| Field | Details |
|---|---|
| **Module** | AI Search / Search Results |
| **Category** | Functional |
| **Severity** | High |
| **Priority** | High |
| **Reproducibility** | 100% |

---

## Preconditions
User is on the platform and has access to the AI Search functionality

---

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the web application
2. Enter "Samsung S23" in the AI search bar
3. Click on the search icon or press Enter
4. Observe the search results

---

## Expected Result
Search results should prioritize and display only Samsung S23-related products. Products from other models such as S25 and S26 should not be included or should be significantly deprioritized

---

## Actual Result
Search results include products from other Samsung models such as S25 and S26, which are not relevant to the specified query

---

## Impact
> When a user searches for a specific product model, receiving 
> irrelevant results damages trust in the AI system and reduces search 
> efficiency. For a B2B e-commerce platform where accurate search is critical 
> to purchasing decisions, this type of AI search inaccuracy can lead to 
> lost sales, user frustration, and reduced confidence in the platform's 
> intelligent search capabilities.
